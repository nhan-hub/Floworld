ABOUT


The world is becoming more and more reliant to digital media and services. Business, social interactions , and entertainment has relied more technology than it had 20 years ago. Social behaviour turning virtual; inciting competition between others for "likes" and the most views on posts instead of experiencing life normally. Can we overcome this digital overload? Or can we adapt and address it?

FloWorld is a light installation that creates a meditative environement for the user. Composed of particles that react to user movement and organic noise, the lights dances along with you in an emersive collaboration of peace and joy.The installation is powered by Processing and uses OpenCV computer vision technology. Particle objects follow an organic noise vector field and will react to movement from the camera image.
