NHAN LE


Nhan Le is a graphic and user experience designer. He is working in the fields of user experience design, game design, and 3D modelling. He has an enthusiasm for exploring the arts with technology and technology as a medium to develop concepts of the digtial and data era of humanity. He is interested in the interpersonal and subconsious effects of living and working with technology that is constantly innovating. He seeks for oppurtunies to design experiences that exceed expectiations of new programs, experiences, games,and services.

In his undergraduate studies at OCAD University, he worked heavily developing web designs, creative coding, and motion designs that commnunicates a unique interaction with traditional design media.
